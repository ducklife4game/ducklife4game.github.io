Return to the origins of duck racings in Duck Life 4! The idea with mutated ducks failed, so now you are getting back to the normal ones, but with curious additions. The game presents an ultimate range of tracks that you will need to accomplish governing little birds across the terrains. Travel from the green lanes to the mouth of the great volcano to defeat the only left champion!

How to Play Duck Life 4

This part of the game offers you to become a coach and train your birds, developing their skills, such as flying, swimming and running. Acquire your first player in Duck Life 4 and spend an appropriate amount of time on practising the basics before venturing for a tournament.

  • Explore all six levels. Starting with peaceful lawns, you are going to navigate through the diverse world of Duck Life 4, defeating adversaries and discovering new tricks and approaches. Each map in the game suggests peculiar barriers, so you will need to accustom to new conditions constantly.
  • Upgrade your runners. In this game you can assemble a whole team of agile ducks, enhancing particular aspects of every bird, preparing them for different tasks. You may even breed the ones you already have to obtain an absolutely new species!
  • Collect the coins. All operations in Duck Life 4 game are done with the help of coins that you may receive in many ways. Win the competitions, accomplish special quests and practice to earn a fortune and acquire the best players and tools.
  • Meet other participants. The more you get accustomed to the village of Duck Life 4, the more ducks will offer you either training or missions. Don’t hesitate to interact with new characters and learn more about the major contest in the game and your greatest enemy.

Your success in Duck Life 4 game depends not only on your speed and agility, but also on the amount of energy you can spend in one go. It also can be increased, as any other characteristic, just let your hardworking bird eat some delicious grapes and it will rush forward with twice as many powers as it had!

Prepare for the Tournament

In Duck Life 4 your main aim is to become the absolute winner of the biggest competition held in the game. However, to get to this point you will need to win a lot of less important contests.

Carefully brace your player by taking lessons from the residents of the farm where you are living and demonstrate the best performance in the decisive moment. In Duck Life 4 you should always be prepared to face exceptional racers!

Investigate the Farm

Apart from learning, Duck Life 4 suggests you discover other activities. Look for the birds that are ready to give you some quest and complete their tasks to earn additional money, experience or even exclusive objects that will help you later.

In between the races the game gives you an opportunity to improve your duck’s characteristics and feed them with rare fruits to give them temporal acceleration. The racings in this game can get pretty vigorous, so you will definitely need some boosts to get to the finish first!
